Reserve A Seat For Your Kid

Help pave the way for your child's bright future by reserving a seat at Ghazali Grammar School, where compassion and education meet under the caring guidance of the Naqshbandi Taleemi Trust. Our school is committed to offering a first-rate education to underprivileged children within a nurturing atmosphere. At Ghazali Grammar School, every student receives the resources and support needed to excel both academically and personally, regardless of their background.

  • Inclusive Education: Our education system embraces diversity, tailored to meet the varied learning needs of each child. We ensure that every student feels supported throughout their educational journey.
  • Holistic Development: We look beyond academic achievements to nurture all aspects of our student's development, enhancing their creative abilities, emotional intelligence, and physical well-being.
  • Community Engagement: Our students learn the importance of community involvement, which instills a sense of responsibility and a commitment to service.

What Happens After You Submit?

Once you have filled out the online seat reservation form, the school management will contact you to provide further information and guide you through the next steps of the admission process. Ensure your contact details are correct to facilitate timely communication.

Admission Dates for 2025

Admissions for the new academic year will commence in February 2025 and continue through June 2025. The new term is scheduled to start in August 2025, following the directives of the Government of Sindh. Please note that these dates are subject to change, and any updates will be announced on our website.
